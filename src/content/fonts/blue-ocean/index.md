Blue Ocean is a variable font forked from VTF Bluu Next and redrawn using quadratic curves. The conversion to quadratic beziers makes it suitable for environments that don't support cubic outlines.

- **Source:** [github.com/eliheuer/blue-ocean](https://github.com/eliheuer/blue-ocean)
